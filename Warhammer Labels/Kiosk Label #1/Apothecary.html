About This Object

In the Imperium of Man, an Apothecary is a medical officer of the Adeptus Astartes. On the battlefield, they treat critical injuries and recover gene-seed from fallen Space Marines. Gene-seed is essential for creating future warriors, making the Apothecary responsible not only for healing, but for preserving the Chapter's future.

This painted miniature highlights the iconic medical tools, battlefield equipment, and solemn authority associated with the role.
