About This Object

Imperial Knights are towering war machines piloted by noble houses, combining ancient tradition with devastating power. Canis Rex is one of the most famous Knights in the setting, known for operating beyond normal House politics and appearing where the Imperium is in crisis.

Gatling Cannon, Reaper Chainsword, and Las-Impulsor are all part of this Knight Preceptor's arsenal. Hekhtur Cerberan, the pilot of Canis Rex, shows no mercy to the enemies utilizing this wide range of weapons.

As a museum object, this miniature shows how the Warhammer hobby handles scale and spectacle. Large models demand different painting choices than infantry. Weathering, metallic surfaces, heraldry, and readable silhouette become key to making the story clear from a distance.
