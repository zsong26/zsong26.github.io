About This Object

A Kill Team is a small, specialised unit built for missions where a full army would be too loud, too slow, or too obvious. In Warhammer 40,000, the Deathwatch are Space Marines seconded from many different Chapters, brought together to eliminate threats that require flexibility, experience, and extreme force in a tight package.

This set represents that idea through variety. Different armour marks, weapons, and insignia signal that each member has a distinct origin and role. As a museum object, it shows how the Warhammer hobby communicates story and identity through small visual choices that viewers can read even before they know the lore.
