About This Object

The Watch Master is a senior commander within the Deathwatch, a Space Marine brotherhood dedicated to combating alien threats. Unlike standard Chapter captains, the Watch Master oversees warriors drawn from many different Chapters, each bringing distinct combat traditions and skills.

Armed with a guardian spear and equipped with advanced tactical gear, the Watch Master directs Kill Teams during high risk missions. As a museum object, this miniature demonstrates how character models in Warhammer communicate hierarchy, authority, and narrative through pose, ornamentation, and weapon design.
